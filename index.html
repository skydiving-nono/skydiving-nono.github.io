What's With the Name?

Written by Anthony Walker on March 19, 2014

Simply, since the first time I've skydived, my life has been changed. There is a beautiful quote from Leonardo da Vinci that pretty much sums it all up:

“Once you have tasted flight, you will forever walk the earth with your eyes turned skyward, for there you have been, and there you will always long to return.”

- Leonardo da Vinci

Now, the ñoño part. In Spanish, ñoño depending on where you are, typically means nerd. Skydiving's cool, I enjoy programming, Yo disfruto español, life's good.
